Transparency Mode: When toggled, adds a canvas-size background to your layer before rendering - yielding better results when dithering a layer with transparent pixels i.e. text or shading layers. Click the color module to switch between a black/white background.
Knockout Mode: When toggled, knocks the background color out of your dithered layer upon render.
Mask Mode: When toggled, dithers only the layer mask of the target layer..
Save Settings: Saves your chosen dither settings for next time.
Color Embue Mode: Renders your dither with the colors of the original layer overlayed.
